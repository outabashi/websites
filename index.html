What PharmaFlow Does

Developed by Future Gate Software S.L., Spain — powering enterprise pharmacy automation across the Middle East and beyond.

PharmaFlow Platform

A comprehensive, end-to-end pharmacy management and automation platform designed to improve patient safety, optimize pharmacy operations, and enable scalable, enterprise-wide smart pharmacy deployments. Hardware-independent, highly configurable, integrates with HIS, ERP, robotic dispensing systems, and third-party healthcare solutions.

PharmaFlow PDM™

A complete, ready-made robotic pharmacy branch delivered as a turnkey solution—designed to be deployed, relocated, and centrally governed at enterprise scale. A pharmacy branch that moves—not a machine that dispenses.

PharmaFlow PDM™

Mobile, Turnkey Self-Service Robotic Pharmacy Branch

A fully enclosed, steel-structure robotic pharmacy branch. Secure, regulation-ready, deployable indoor or outdoor, relocatable, and centrally operated as part of a unified pharmacy network.

Why PharmaFlow

Patient Safety

  • Error-free dispensing through item-level barcode verification
  • Batch and expiry validation against entire treatment duration
  • Full batch and lot traceability down to patient level
  • Controlled and narcotic medication governance with biometric verification
  • Structured patient counseling workflows

Cost Reduction & Resource Optimization

  • Reduces waste by prioritizing near-expiry stock and managing open packs
  • Optimizes inventory with real-time tracking and consumption-based forecasting
  • Automated reorder and replenishment logic
  • Minimizes manual effort with barcode-driven receiving, transfers, and returns
  • Hardware-independent, scalable platform with no vendor lock-in

Patient Experience

  • Shorter waiting times through robotics-optimized dispensing
  • SMS and email notifications for prescription status and pickup readiness
  • e-Refill requests, delivery management, and omnichannel workflows
  • Digital acknowledgment, e-signatures, and counseling documentation
  • Consistent, reliable service across all pharmacy locations

Automated Workflow Efficiency

  • Orchestrates robotic and non-robotic dispensing in a single workflow
  • Load balancing across counters and robots to eliminate bottlenecks
  • Automated prescription grouping, queue management, and task prioritization
  • Real-time visibility into workload, robot status, and dispensing progress
  • Frees pharmacists to focus on clinical tasks rather than manual processing

Compliance, Governance & Audit Readiness

  • End-to-end traceability from supplier to patient
  • Comprehensive audit trails for users, transactions, and dispensing events
  • Digital signatures and paperless workflows
  • Controlled dispensing policies, segregation of duties, role-based access
  • Regulatory-ready reporting and dashboards for inspections and audits

Scalability & Enterprise Performance

  • Standardized pharmacy operations across multiple sites
  • Faster rollout of new pharmacies or services
  • Lower IT complexity and integration risk
  • Supports single sites, multi-hospital groups, and national networks
  • High availability architecture without re-engineering
