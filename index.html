The Platform

PharmaFlow™

The digital backbone of automated pharmacy operations. A comprehensive end-to-end pharmacy management system capable of running complete outpatient and inpatient pharmacy operations—enabling seamless outsourcing of pharmacy services with controlled workflows, role-based access, and KPI reporting under the provider’s governance.

  • Dispensing governance, clinical decision support & medication safety
  • Inventory, supply chain & formulary management
  • Insurance, payments & financial reconciliation
  • Enterprise dashboards, BI reporting & compliance
  • HIS/EMR integration, national platform connectivity & SSO

PharmaFlow™ PDM™

Mobile, Turnkey Self-Service Robotic Pharmacy Branch

A fully enclosed, steel-structure robotic pharmacy branch. PharmaFlow™ manages networks of robotic pharmacy branches, not individual machines—secure, regulation-ready, deployable indoor or outdoor, relocatable, and centrally operated as part of a unified pharmacy network.
