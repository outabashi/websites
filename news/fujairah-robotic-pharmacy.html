Milestone January 12, 2017

The Future Robotic Pharmacy Operating for First Time in Fujairah Hospital, UAE

Back to Newsroom

FutureGate Software completed an innovative project automating outpatient pharmacy services at Fujairah Hospital using the PharmaFlow solution, fully integrated with BD Rowa Vmax robots.

The robotic system processes medications at high speeds: up to 8-12 seconds per one process of medication refill and up to 2,000 processes of pharmaceutical packs delivery per hour.

The platform combines drug dispensing robotics and workflow within a unified architecture, offering pharmacies comprehensive software capabilities. PharmaFlow delivers industrial strength software tools necessary to handle the complexities of modern prescription processing, robot automation, workflow, inventory and queue management — increasing efficiency and patient safety.
