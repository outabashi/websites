Al Wakra Hospital Outpatient Pharmacy Automation

Back to Newsroom

The initiative aimed to establish Al Wakra Outpatient Pharmacy as one of the most automated hospital pharmacies in Qatar and the Middle East.

The project implemented several key technological improvements:

  • Dispensing robot installation for medication distribution
  • Queue management system to organize patient flow
  • Workflow revisions to streamline pharmacy operations
  • Medication barcode system for inventory and accuracy tracking

Results Achieved

Reduced patient waiting times

Improved stock management capabilities

Increased patient satisfaction levels

The automation project represents a comprehensive modernization effort combining robotic automation with digital tracking systems to enhance overall pharmacy efficiency and patient experience at the Al Wakra Hospital facility.
