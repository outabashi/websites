PharmaFlow™ Platform

Designed for Complex Healthcare Environments

PharmaFlow™ orchestrates medication workflows across outpatient and inpatient settings by unifying systems, automating processes, and enforcing governance—without disrupting existing infrastructure. It provides enterprise-level control, visibility, and intelligence across dispensing, inventory, patients, billing, insurance, and performance.

Platform Overview

PharmaFlow™ is a comprehensive, end-to-end pharmacy management and automation platform. Hardware-independent, highly configurable. Delivers a unified, safe, and future-ready pharmacy ecosystem enabling healthcare organizations to reduce medication errors, optimize inventory, improve operational efficiency, and scale smart pharmacy services.

Why PharmaFlow™

Patient Safety & Clinical Decision Support

  • Full Medi-Span CDSS integration for clinical verification
  • Real-time drug interaction, allergy, and contraindication screening
  • Dose, age, and renal-adjusted verification
  • Item-level barcode verification with batch and expiry validation
  • Full batch traceability down to patient level
  • Controlled and narcotic medication governance with biometric verification

Cost Reduction & Resource Optimization

  • Reduces waste by prioritizing near-expiry stock and managing open packs
  • Optimizes inventory with real-time tracking and consumption-based forecasting
  • Automated reorder and replenishment logic
  • Minimizes manual effort with barcode-driven receiving, transfers, and returns
  • Hardware-independent, scalable platform with no vendor lock-in

Patient Experience

  • Shorter waiting times through robotics-optimized dispensing
  • SMS and email notifications for prescription status and pickup readiness
  • e-Refill requests, delivery management, and omnichannel workflows
  • Digital acknowledgment, e-signatures, and counseling documentation
  • Consistent, reliable service across all pharmacy locations

Automated Workflow Efficiency

  • Orchestrates robotic and non-robotic dispensing in a single workflow
  • Load balancing across counters and robots to eliminate bottlenecks
  • Automated prescription grouping, queue management, and task prioritization
  • Real-time visibility into workload, robot status, and dispensing progress

Formulary & Terminology Governance

Enterprise-grade drug catalog and clinical terminology management

Centralized Formulary Repository

Approved generic and branded medications organized by therapeutic class with version control and change tracking.

HIS Code Mapping & GTIN/NDC

Configurable per-facility code mapping with GTIN/GS1 identifiers and National Drug Codes for accurate dispensing and traceability.

Unified Terminology Database

SNOMED CT, ICD-10, and LOINC with registry integration for terminology updates and role-based access with full audit trail.

Order Templates & Care Plans

Pre-built order sentences and diagnosis-based order sets aligned to evidence-based care protocols.

Brand & Substitution Management

Formulary, brand, strength, pack-size, and substitution rules with version history to reduce outdated prescribing.

Item Pack Images

Medication pack images linked to each formulary item for visual verification by pharmacists during dispensing and by patients at PDM™ self-service kiosks.

Clinical Decision Support

  • HIS-linked medication reconciliation
  • Automated pre-authorization workflows
