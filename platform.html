PharmaFlow Platform

Enterprise Control Layer for a Network of Robotic Pharmacy Branches

The PharmaFlow platform centrally governs and operates a distributed network of PharmaFlow PDM™ mobile robotic pharmacy branches, providing enterprise-level control, visibility, and intelligence across dispensing, inventory, patients, payments, insurance, and performance.

Platform Overview

PharmaFlow™ is a comprehensive, end-to-end pharmacy management and automation platform. Hardware-independent, highly configurable. Delivers a unified, safe, and future-ready pharmacy ecosystem enabling healthcare organizations to reduce medication errors, optimize inventory, improve operational efficiency, and scale smart pharmacy services.

Enterprise Network Management

Designed to operate pharmacy networks, not individual machines. A single PharmaFlow instance centrally manages multiple PDM™ units, across multiple locations, under unified governance, policies, and workflows.
