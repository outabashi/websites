Privacy Policy

How FutureGate Software S.L. collects, uses, protects, and manages data through the PharmaFlow™ platform.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

FutureGate Software S.L. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) develops and operates the PharmaFlow™ platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you visit our website or when our platform is used by our clients. We act as a data processor on behalf of our clients, who remain the data controllers for all health-related data.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect contact information submitted through our website, standard web analytics data, platform user credentials and activity logs, and health-related data processed on behalf of our clients (patient records, prescriptions, dispensing data). Patient data is owned and controlled by the healthcare organization.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used to operate the platform as instructed by our clients, integrate with national platforms as configured by our clients (e.g., NPHIES, Raqeeb, Wasfaty in KSA; Tatmeen in UAE), maintain security and audit trails, improve platform performance through de-identified analytics, and respond to website inquiries.

4. Data Protection

We implement enterprise-grade security including encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit), role-based access control, tamper-proof audit logging, and strict data isolation in multi-tenant deployments. See our Security & Compliance page for details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Data may only be shared as directed by our clients, submitted to national platforms as configured by our clients, exchanged through Health Information Exchanges configured by the client, or disclosed when required by legal obligation.

6. Data Retention & Your Rights

Retention periods are determined by our clients in accordance with applicable laws. Upon termination of a client agreement, data is returned or securely destroyed.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. For patient data requests, please contact the healthcare organization that manages your care. For website-related inquiries, contact us directly.

7. Cookies & Updates

Our website may use essential cookies for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. This policy may be updated from time to time; the date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@pharmaflow.es or use our contact form.
