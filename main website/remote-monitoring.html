Remote Patient Monitoring

Vendor-Agnostic Critical Care Surveillance

A comprehensive remote monitoring solution that captures real-time data from bedside medical devices regardless of manufacturer. Powered by OSSI Medical Data Governance (MDG), our platform delivers AI-ready patient data with blockchain-secured integrity for critical care and emergency medical services.

OSSI Medical Data Governance (MDG)

The intelligent backbone of our remote monitoring ecosystem. OSSI MDG captures, normalizes, and governs medical device data in real time—transforming raw signals into actionable clinical intelligence.

Real-Time Data Capture

Continuously captures vital signs, waveforms, and alarm data from bedside monitors, ventilators, infusion pumps, and other medical devices—regardless of vendor or protocol.

Vendor-Agnostic Architecture

Works with any medical device manufacturer—Philips, GE, Drager, Mindray, and more. No proprietary lock-in, giving facilities complete freedom in their device procurement strategy.

AI-Ready Data Governance

Structured, normalized data pipelines ready for AI and machine learning analytics. Enable predictive alerts, trend analysis, and clinical decision support from day one.

Blockchain-Secured Integrity

Every data point is cryptographically secured with blockchain technology, ensuring tamper-proof audit trails, regulatory compliance, and unquestionable data provenance.

EMS Remote Monitoring

Extending critical care monitoring beyond hospital walls. Our EMS solution enables real-time patient data transmission from ambulances and field units to receiving facilities.

Live Data Transmission

Real-time vital signs, ECG waveforms, and patient data streamed from ambulances to emergency departments before arrival.

Pre-Arrival Preparation

Emergency teams receive patient data in advance, enabling preparation of resources, specialists, and treatment plans before the patient arrives.

Remote Physician Guidance

Specialists can remotely guide paramedics through complex procedures using real-time data visualization and two-way communication.

Key Benefits

Real-Time Patient Data

Continuous streaming of vital signs and clinical parameters from any bedside device in real time.

AI-Ready Data Governance

Structured data pipelines enable machine learning models for predictive analytics and early warning systems.

Blockchain-Secured Integrity

Immutable data records with cryptographic verification for regulatory compliance and legal defensibility.
