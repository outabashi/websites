FutureGate & Cigalah Group Partner to Expand Healthcare Technology Across Saudi Arabia

FutureGate Partners with RT Healthtech for AI Rehabilitation Robotics in MENA

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions has entered an exclusive distribution partnership with RT Healthtech to bring Hopebotics’ clinically validated AI-driven wearable rehabilitation robotics — HandTasker, XoBrace, and XoMuscle — to hospitals across the MENA region.

February 2024 — Arab Health Exhibition, Dubai

EHS, BHealthCare & FutureGate Sign MOU for Robotic Blood Collection

During Arab Health 2024, Emirates Health Services (EHS), BHealthCare, and FutureGate Healthcare Solutions signed a Memorandum of Understanding to develop robotic blood collection services using HEIVA autonomous technology. The agreement aims to position the UAE as the first country to implement robotic blood drawing technology.

Read more

December 2023

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions Partners with OSSI for AI-Powered Medical Data Governance

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions has entered a strategic partnership with OneSource Solutions International (OSSI) to bring Medical Data Governance (MDG) and vendor-agnostic remote patient monitoring, TeleICU, and ambulance telehealth solutions to healthcare institutions across the MENA region.

February 2023

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions Signs MOU with Emirates Health Services (EHS)

FutureGate signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Emirates Health Services for cooperation in pharmaceutical services innovation, AI technologies, and robotic self-service pharmacies.

April 2022

PharmaFlow Self-Service Kiosks Launch at Ibrahim Obaidullah Hospital

e-Prescription preparation and dispensing via PharmaFlow self-service kiosks and BD Rowa robotic machines successfully launched at Ibrahim Bin Hamad Obaidullah Hospital in Ras Al Khaimah, UAE.

March 2022

EHS Adds Four Robotic Pharmacies and Smart Drug Self-Dispensing Systems

Emirates Health Services launched robotic pharmacies at Al Qassimi, Al Qassimi Women’s, Ibrahim Obaidullah, and Abdullah Bin Omran hospitals — with storage capacity exceeding 45,000 medicine boxes and automatic prescription preparation in under one minute.

Read on ehs.gov.ae

March 2021

Distribution Agreement with Becton Dickinson Rowa Germany (BD)

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions announced a distribution agreement with Becton Dickinson Rowa Germany GmbH (BD) for pharmacy automation solutions in the United Arab Emirates.

January 2017

First Robotic Pharmacy in Fujairah Hospital, UAE

FutureGate completed an innovative outpatient pharmacy automation project at Fujairah Hospital using PharmaFlow integrated with BD Rowa Vmax technology — the first of its kind in the UAE.
