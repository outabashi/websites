The Bridge to Regulated Intelligence

Govern, regulate, and synchronize clinical data from any source — making it trusted, structured, and AI-ready. The foundation every AI application in healthcare needs.

Request a Demo

The Challenge

AI Without Governed Data Is Dangerous

Healthcare organizations across the MENA region and beyond are rushing to adopt AI — from clinical decision support to predictive analytics, from automated reporting to intelligent alerting.

But there is a critical problem: without governed data, AI engines and LLMs hallucinate, produce inaccurate clinical outputs, and create patient safety risks.

Most clinical data today is fragmented across dozens of devices, disparate systems, and incompatible formats. It is uncurated, unverified, and unreliable — making it a dangerous foundation for any AI application that touches patient care.

Data Fragmentation

Clinical data scattered across medical devices, HIS, EMR, and dozens of incompatible systems with no single source of truth.

AI Hallucination Risk

Unregulated, unverified data fed to AI engines and LLMs leads to false clinical insights, incorrect diagnoses, and dangerous treatment recommendations.

Compliance Gaps

Ungoverned data fails regulatory requirements, exposes institutions to legal risk, and cannot meet the audit trail demands of modern healthcare compliance.

The Solution

OSSI MDG® — Medical Data Governance

MDG® is a data governance layer that sits between clinical data sources and AI applications. It captures, synchronizes, normalizes, and regulates clinical data from any source — ensuring only verified, governed data reaches AI engines, LLMs, and clinical applications.

What MDG® Delivers

Data Synchronization

Real-time synchronization of clinical data across heterogeneous systems and devices — ensuring every data point is current, consistent, and available where it is needed.

Data Normalization

Standardize formats, codes, and units across all data sources into a canonical model — eliminating inconsistencies and enabling interoperability at scale.

Data Regulation

Enforce governance policies, access controls, and compliance rules on every data point — ensuring clinical data meets the highest standards of safety and privacy.

Source Verification

Validate and authenticate data provenance to ensure trustworthiness — every data point is traced back to its origin with full chain-of-custody documentation.

AI-Ready Output

Deliver clean, structured, governed data ready for AI, ML, and LLM applications — eliminating hallucination risk and enabling trustworthy clinical intelligence.

Audit & Compliance

Complete audit trail with immutable logging for regulatory compliance — meeting the demands of healthcare regulators, accreditation bodies, and institutional governance.
