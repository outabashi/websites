Solutions

Self-Service Robotic Pharmacy

Fully automated, standalone pharmacy branches that dispense medications 24/7 — designed for outdoor and indoor environments with a seamless, patient-centric experience.

Overview Models Workflow Patient Centricity Integration

Overview

Pharmacy Without Boundaries

The Self-Service Robotic Pharmacy is a fully autonomous, standalone pharmacy branch that operates around the clock without the need for on-site pharmacists. It combines advanced robotics, intelligent software, and patient-centric design to deliver a complete pharmacy experience in compact, deployable units.

Whether placed in a hospital lobby, shopping mall, metro station, or residential community, the robotic pharmacy brings medication dispensing directly to patients — handling prescriptions, over-the-counter products, and online order pickups with speed and accuracy.

Two Models

Designed for Every Environment

Two purpose-built models address distinct deployment scenarios, each engineered for its target environment.

MODEL A — OUTDOOR

Outdoor Deployment

A weather-resistant, standalone unit designed for outdoor placement in public areas, parking lots, residential compounds, and community spaces. Built to withstand extreme temperatures, dust, and humidity.

  • Public areas & parking facilities
  • Residential communities & compounds
  • Climate-controlled enclosure
  • 24/7 unattended operation
MODEL B — INDOOR

Indoor Deployment

A sleek, compact unit designed for high-traffic indoor locations such as metro stations, shopping malls, hospital lobbies, and commercial buildings. Integrates seamlessly into existing spaces.

  • Metro stations & transit hubs
  • Shopping malls & commercial buildings
  • Compact footprint design
  • Seamless architectural integration

Workflow

Simple, Intuitive & User-Friendly

The robotic pharmacy delivers a frictionless experience for every type of medication request — prescriptions, over-the-counter products, and online order pickups.

1

Prescription Dispensing

Patients scan their prescription or enter their ID. The system verifies the order, the robot retrieves the medication, and dispenses it securely through a controlled door.

2

OTC Products

Browse available over-the-counter medications on the touchscreen, select products, complete payment, and collect items from the dispensing point — all in under a minute.

3

Online Order Pickup

Orders placed online or via mobile app are pre-prepared by the robot. Patients simply authenticate at the kiosk and collect their pre-packaged order instantly.

Patient Centricity

Designed Around the Patient

Every detail is engineered to ensure a safe, accessible, and comfortable experience for all users.

Controlled Door System

Secure dispensing doors that only open upon verified authentication, ensuring medications are delivered safely and privately to the correct patient.

Multiple Payment Options

Supports credit/debit cards, mobile payments, insurance integration, and contactless NFC — giving patients flexibility in how they pay.

Multi-Language Interface

Touchscreen interface available in multiple languages with intuitive navigation, ensuring accessibility for diverse patient populations.

E-Signature & Consent

Digital signature capture for regulatory compliance, prescription acknowledgment, and consent documentation — fully paperless and audit-ready.

Integration

Seamless System Connectivity

The robotic pharmacy integrates with your existing healthcare infrastructure to deliver a unified, connected experience.

HIS Integration

Connects with Hospital Information Systems for real-time prescription data, patient records, and clinical workflows.

ERP Systems

Integrates with enterprise resource planning for inventory management, procurement, and financial reconciliation.

Billing & Insurance

Automated billing, insurance claim processing, and payment gateway integration for seamless financial transactions.

Dispensing Robotics

Direct connectivity with robotic dispensing hardware for real-time inventory tracking, automated picking, and status monitoring.
