OSSI Medical Middleware

Medical Device Data Connectivity

Connecting Clinical Devices to Healthcare Systems

OSSI provides medical middleware connecting over 90% of medical machines used for intensive care, bridging the gap between clinical devices and information systems. Purpose-built to handle the complexity of multi-vendor device ecosystems, our connectivity platform ensures accurate, real-time clinical data flows seamlessly from bedside to electronic medical records.

What It Does

OSSI connects to medical devices across multiple manufacturers and specialties. Acting as medical middleware, it handles data transformation, proprietary protocols, and integration—delivering clean, standardized data ready for clinical system use.

Multi-Vendor Connectivity

Connect to 90%+ of ICU devices across all major manufacturers, eliminating vendor lock-in and enabling a unified view of patient data from any bedside device.

Data Transformation

Handles proprietary protocols from dozens of device manufacturers and transforms raw device output into standardized, clinically meaningful data ready for use.

Network Protection

Manages security and network protection for connected medical devices, isolating clinical networks and safeguarding sensitive patient data in transit.

Real-Time Integration

Direct, real-time connectivity with patient monitors, ventilators, anesthesia machines, and more—ensuring clinical teams always have the latest device data.

Regulatory & Quality

OSSI Medical Device Data Connectivity is built on a foundation of regulatory compliance and quality assurance, meeting the highest standards for medical device data systems.

FDA Quality System

Compliant with FDA Medical Device Quality System regulations, ensuring rigorous design controls, documentation, and manufacturing processes for medical-grade software.

MDDS Classification

Classified under Medical Device Data Systems (MDDS) regulatory framework, providing a clear pathway for deployment in clinical environments with appropriate risk categorization.

Risk Mitigation

Comprehensive risk mitigation protocols ensuring data accuracy, completeness, and integrity throughout the entire data chain from device capture to clinical system delivery.
