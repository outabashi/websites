Connecting Healthcare Ecosystems

System Integrations

Seamless Healthcare IT Connectivity

Healthcare IT integration that connects clinical and operational systems into a unified, interoperable ecosystem. We bridge the gap between disparate platforms—HIS, ERP, billing, patient portals, and national health networks—enabling seamless data flow, smarter decision-making, and regulatory compliance.

Unified Healthcare Connectivity

Modern healthcare facilities rely on dozens of interconnected systems. Our integration services ensure every system speaks the same language, delivering real-time data where it is needed most.

HIS Integration

Seamless connectivity with Hospital Information Systems, ensuring clinical data flows effortlessly between departments, from admissions to discharge and beyond.

ERP Connectivity

Link enterprise resource planning systems with clinical operations for unified financial management, supply chain visibility, and operational efficiency.

Billing System Integration

Automate revenue cycle processes by integrating billing, insurance claims, and payment systems for accurate, timely reimbursement and financial transparency.

Patient Portal Connections

Empower patients with secure access to their health records, appointment scheduling, prescription management, and communication with care teams.

National Health Platforms

Full connectivity with government health platforms including NPHIES, Wasfaty, Tatmeen, Raqeeb, and Health Information Exchanges (HIE) for regulatory compliance.

API & Standards

Industry-standard protocols including HL7, FHIR, REST, and SOAP APIs ensure reliable, secure, and future-proof data exchange across all connected systems.

National Health Platform Connectivity

We specialize in connecting healthcare facilities to regional and national digital health ecosystems, ensuring compliance and seamless data sharing.

N

NPHIES

National Platform for Health Insurance Exchange Services—real-time insurance eligibility, prior authorization, and claims processing.

W

Wasfaty

Electronic prescription platform enabling secure transmission of prescriptions from healthcare providers to community pharmacies.

T

Tatmeen

Pharmaceutical track-and-trace system ensuring supply chain integrity, authenticity verification, and regulatory compliance for medications.

R

Raqeeb

Monitoring and oversight platform for healthcare facility compliance, quality assurance, and regulatory reporting requirements.

HIE

Health Information Exchange

Secure exchange of patient health information across organizations, enabling coordinated care and a unified patient record.

Custom Integrations

Beyond standard platforms, we build custom integrations for any regional or proprietary health information systems your facility requires.

Integration Standards & Protocols

HL7

HL7 Messaging

Health Level Seven standard messaging for clinical data exchange, including ADT, ORM, ORU, and SIU message types for comprehensive interoperability.

FHIR

FHIR Resources

Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources enabling modern, RESTful APIs for granular health data exchange, supporting the latest interoperability standards.

API

REST & SOAP APIs

Flexible API integration supporting both RESTful and SOAP web services, ensuring compatibility with legacy and modern healthcare systems alike.

Our Integration Approach

A structured, proven methodology that minimizes risk and maximizes value at every stage of the integration lifecycle.

1

Requirements Gathering

Comprehensive assessment of existing systems, workflows, and integration needs to define clear objectives and scope.

2

System Mapping

Detailed mapping of data flows, endpoints, and dependencies to architect a robust and scalable integration framework.

3

Data Transformation

Normalize and transform data across systems, ensuring consistency, accuracy, and compliance with healthcare standards.

4

Testing & Validation
