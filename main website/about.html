About FutureGate Healthcare Solutions

Company Overview

A leading provider of turnkey, state-of-the-art healthcare IT solutions — robotics, AI, automation, and digital health — across the MENA region.

Who We Are

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions L.L.C.

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions is a leading provider of turnkey and state-of-the-art intelligent and innovative healthcare solutions. Headquartered in Dubai, UAE, we serve clients across 5 countries in the MENA region, anticipating industry trends and developing customer-focused solutions that drive real impact.

We partner with healthcare institutions to deliver automation, digital platforms, and smart clinical solutions that enhance operational efficiency, improve patient safety, and reduce costs.

Under the leadership of CEO Osama Odabashi, FutureGate brings over 25 years of experience serving clients across 5 countries, with 3 international white papers published, establishing a strong reputation for innovation and excellence in healthcare technology.

FutureGate Healthcare Solutions is a division of FutureGate Group — a multi-sector enterprise headquartered in Doha, Qatar, with operations spanning business services, software development, healthcare, real estate, and trading across the MENA region.
