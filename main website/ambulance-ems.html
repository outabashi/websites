OneSource Connect EMS

In today's world, telemedicine and staff shortages are increasing the demand for streamlined communication and easy remote consultations.

Overview

Connecting Ambulances to Remote Clinicians

OneSource Connect EMS bridges the gap between ambulance crews and remote clinical teams, enabling real-time data sharing, live video streaming, and instant consultations that accelerate patient care and improve outcomes.

Accelerate Patient Care

Share critical data and real-time status to accelerate clinical decision-making and patient care.

Organize Workflow

Organize your workflow across all teams with live streaming and synchronized data views.

Expedite Treatment

Expedite treatment or transport plans backed by remote clinicians with full visibility into patient status.

Streamline Communication

Streamline communication with easy access to patient updates across the entire care chain.

Core Capabilities

Key Features That Power EMS Telehealth

A comprehensive platform that streams, records, and shares real-time medical data between ambulances and remote clinical teams.

Real-Time Data Streaming

Stream and synchronize real-time medical device data, waveforms and video from multiple sources (monitor, defibrillator, infusion pumps, ventilator) with up to 2 IP video cameras. Requires 4G/5G connectivity.

Remote Access

Remote viewing of combined data, waveforms and video through secure web browser. Up to 5 concurrent remote viewers per ambulance for collaborative clinical decision-making.

Ambulance Integration

Fully integrated into ambulance infrastructure with local wireless web server and cloud service for seamless connectivity and data management.

24-Hour Recording

Records and stores up to 24 hours of continuous synchronized data with DVR-like controls including pause, fast forward, rewind, and live viewing.

Lossless Capture

Lossless data capture even when there is no cellular connectivity, ensuring no critical patient information is ever lost during transport.

Multi-Ambulance Mode

Supervisor mode allows viewing multiple ambulances simultaneously, giving dispatchers and medical directors a complete operational overview.
