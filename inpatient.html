Dispensing & Medication Preparation

Pharmacists dispense verified orders from multiple pharmacy locations based on availability and working hours. The preparation workflow generates structured worklists and prints medication labels with 2D barcodes—essential for accurate bedside medication verification.

Stock is reserved at the point of dispensing and automatically deducted from pharmacy inventory upon confirmed ward delivery, maintaining real-time accuracy at batch level.

Cycle Fill — Automated Dose Calculation

The system automatically calculates all required doses for every active order across all wards and generates dispense records for them. This eliminates manual dose counting and order-by-order processing, allowing pharmacists in the central or inpatient pharmacy to focus on clinical tasks instead of repetitive dispensing calculations.

Ward Delivery & Medication Administration

Secure medication transfer from pharmacy to ward, followed by tracked bedside administration by nursing staff.

Ward Delivery

  • Delivery reports with all prepared items for accountability
  • Mandatory nurse verification before delivery confirmation
  • Auto stock update: decrease pharmacy, increase ward at batch level
  • Auto-reserve delivered stock per patient until administration

Medication Administration

  • Manual dose administration by ward nurse with stock tracking
  • Auto-administration via HIS integration when supported
  • Bedside barcode verification for correct medication and patient
  • Real-time ward stock adjustment upon each administered dose

Ward Inventory & Stock Management

Complete control over inpatient pharmacy and ward-level stock, including requisitions, transfers, batch management, and returns.

Requisition & Receiving

Create stock requisition orders, filling, and receiving for inpatient wards with full traceability.

Transfers & Adjustments

Stock transfers between wards and central pharmacy, physical inventory, and inventory adjustments.

Batch & Expiry Management

Track medication batches and expiry dates. Transfer expired stock to the expiry store for proper handling.

On-Hand Stock Visibility

View real-time on-hand stock for all items in every ward. Trace all stock transactions at batch level.

Returns & Waste

Release reserved stock for discontinued orders or patient discharge. Track wasted stock for inventory adjustment.

Automated Dispensing Cabinet Integration

Stock Actions

Restock, destock, remove, and return actions processed automatically between PharmaFlow and the ADC cabinet.

Barcode Scanning

Manufacturer barcode scanning on stock receive. Barcode included in HL7 messages and verified at ADC cabinet level.

Auto-Replenishment

Auto-create cabinet stock requisition orders based on defined Min/Max item quantity thresholds per medication and consumable category.

Consumable Items

Full support for non-medication consumable items in formulary, stock management, and ADC cabinet dispensing.

Multi-Barcode Designs

Support different barcode designs per site location and item category. Print barcodes for medications and non-medication items.

Dispense from Cabinet

Define Rx order dispensing from ADC cabinet for the whole quantity or initial doses directly from the clinical verification or dispense screens.
