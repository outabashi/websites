PharmaFlow™ PDM™

Mobile, Turnkey Self-Service Robotic Pharmacy Branch

A complete, ready-made robotic pharmacy branch delivered as a turnkey solution—designed to be deployed, relocated, and centrally governed at enterprise scale.

What PharmaFlow™ PDM™ Is

A fully enclosed, steel-structure robotic pharmacy branch, professionally designed and governed by the PharmaFlow™ platform.

  • Secure and regulation-ready
  • Deployable indoor or outdoor
  • Relocatable based on business needs
  • Centrally operated as part of a network
  • Located in public locations, accessible 24/7
  • Supports drive-thru model

“A pharmacy branch that moves—not a machine that dispenses.”

Drive-Thru Support

PharmaFlow™ PDM™ supports outdoor and drive-thru deployment configurations, enabling convenient medication access from vehicles—extending pharmacy services to parking areas, hospital campuses, and high-traffic locations.

Innovative Service Offerings

Three integrated service models powered by the PDM™ network

Medication Refill Dispensing & Delivery

  • Provide a convenient way to dispense and deliver medications to patients
  • Located in public locations, accessible 24/7, with drive-thru support
  • Fulfillment and distribution center to supply the network and package prescriptions for home delivery
  • Easy to use with convenient self-service experience

Home Delivery

  • Medications delivered directly and safely to a patient’s home
  • Bypassing the need to visit the hospital or pharmacy
  • Targeting high-volume delivery operations per year
  • Verify, dispense, and package prescriptions for delivery from a centralized fulfillment center

Patient Engagement

  • Counseling: Providing vital information, advice, and assistance to enhance the patient’s understanding of their illness and treatment
  • Education: Help patients understand what medications they are taking and why, how and when to take them, and recognize side effects
  • Follow-up: Routine follow-up of patients with chronic disease. Regular monitoring and Rx-Refill approvals via telehealth platform
  • Improve adherence and therapeutic outcomes
