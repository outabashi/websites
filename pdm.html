What PharmaFlow™ PDM™ Is

A fully enclosed, steel-structure robotic pharmacy branch, professionally designed and governed by the PharmaFlow™ platform.

  • Secure and regulation-ready
  • Deployable indoor or outdoor
  • Relocatable based on business needs
  • Centrally operated as part of a network
  • Located in public locations, accessible 24/7
  • Supports drive-thru model

“A pharmacy branch that moves—not a machine that dispenses.”

Drive-Thru Support

PharmaFlow™ PDM™ supports outdoor and drive-thru deployment configurations, enabling convenient medication access from vehicles—extending pharmacy services to parking areas, hospital campuses, and high-traffic locations.
